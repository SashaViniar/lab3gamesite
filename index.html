Uter

Uther Lightbringer is the standard hero for paladins. Players who have unlocked the Lady Liadrin alternate hero may choose to play as her instead when using any paladin deck. Both heroes use the same gameplay mechanics; the difference is purely cosmetic

The paladin Hero Power is Reinforce, which summons a 1/1 Silver Hand Recruit. This provides a cheap and unending supply of weak minions, which can be improved using spells like Blessing of Kings, or simply used to bolster the player's forces.

Garrosh

Garrosh Hellscream is the standard hero for warriors. Players who have unlocked the Magni Bronzebeard alternate hero may choose to play as him instead when using any warrior deck. Both heroes use the same gameplay mechanics; the difference is purely cosmetic.

Armor Up! is the warrior Hero Power, allowing the warrior to gain 2 Armor.

Anduin

Anduin Wrynn is the standard hero for priests. Players who have unlocked the Tyrande Whisperwind alternate hero may choose to play as her instead when using any priest deck. Both heroes use the same gameplay mechanics; the difference is purely cosmetic.

The default priest Hero Power is Lesser Heal, a powerful healing option which can be used on heroes or minions to restore Health.

Valeera

Rogues are backstabbing assassins, known for their stealth, speed and deadly efficiency. Masters of deadly poisons and hidden strikes, most victims of rogues never see their attacker coming.

The rogue Hero Power offers some ability to maintain board control, as it allows your hero to destroy 1-Health minions.

Warlock

In the face of demonic power, most heroes see death. Warlocks see only opportunity. Dominance is their aim, and they have found a path to it in the dark arts. These voracious spellcasters summon demonic minions to fight beside them.

At first, they command only the service of imps, but as a warlock’s knowledge grows, seductive succubi, loyal voidwalkers, and horrific felhunters join the dark sorcerer’s ranks to wreak havoc on anyone who stands in their master’s way.

Druid

Druids harness the vast powers of nature to preserve balance and protect life. With experience, druids can unleash nature’s raw energy against their enemies, raining celestial fury on them from a great distance, binding them with enchanted vines, or ensnaring them in unrelenting cyclones.

Druids have a small synergy with Beasts, although much less so than hunters.

Trall

Thrall is the standard hero for shamans. Players who have unlocked the Morgl the Oracle alternate hero may choose to play as him instead when using any shaman deck. Both heroes use the same gameplay mechanics; the difference is purely cosmetic

Hero Powers are unique abilities, specific to each class, usable used once each turn.

Jayna

Jaina Proudmoore is the standard hero for mages. Players who have unlocked the Khadgar or Medivh alternate heroes may choose to play as one of them instead when using any mage deck. All heroes use the same gameplay mechanics; the difference is purely cosmetic.

Hero Powers are unique abilities, specific to each class. Hero Powers cost 2 mana, and can be used once per turn.

Rexxar

Rexxar is the standard hero for hunters. Players who have unlocked the Alleria Windrunner alternate hero may choose to play as her instead when using any hunter deck. Both heroes use the same gameplay mechanics; the difference is purely cosmetic.

From an early age the call of the wild draws some adventurers from the comfort of their homes into the unforgiving primal world outside.
